Our Story: Celebrating 50 Years in Fang District, Chiang Mai

Koon Mow Orange Farm has been a cornerstone of agriculture in Fang District, Chiang Mai, for over 50 years. Founded in 1974, our journey began with a simple dream: to cultivate the best oranges in the region. Inspired by the lush landscapes and the potential of our local soil, our founder dedicated themselves to sustainable farming practices that honor both nature and community. Throughout the years, we have achieved significant milestones, from expanding our orchards to implementing innovative techniques that enhance quality and yield. Our commitment to excellence has allowed us to grow while staying true to our values. Our Mission

At Koonmow Farm, our mission is to grow fresh, healthy, and sustainably produced products while making a positive impact on the environment and community. Through eco-friendly farming practices, we aim to preserve the land for future generations and deliver the highest quality goods. We believe in fostering a connection between nature and our customers, promoting well-being, and supporting a healthier lifestyle. By prioritizing sustainability, innovation, and community engagement, we strive to set a standard for responsible farming that benefits both the earth and the people we serve.

Our values

Quality

Every product from Orange Farm is carefully cultivated and processed to meet the highest standards. We believe that quality and care go hand-in-hand, and we are dedicated to delivering only the best.

Sustainability

We are committed to farming practices that protect the environment and promote long-term ecological balance. By minimizing waste and utilizing eco-friendly techniques, we ensure that our land thrives for generations to come.

Transparency

We believe in being open about our processes. From our organic farming methods to our partnerships, we prioritize transparency to build trust with our customers.
