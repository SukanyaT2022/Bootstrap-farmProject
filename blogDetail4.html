Delicious Drink Recipes Using Sai Nam Peung Oranges

Few fruits can match the vibrant sweetness and tangy zest of Sai Nam Peung oranges.

Sai Nam Peung oranges are renowned for their juicy sweetness and fragrant aroma, making them a perfect ingredient for refreshing beverages. Whether you're entertaining guests, looking for a healthy alternative to store-bought drinks, or simply craving something delicious, these recipes will inspire you to make the most of this exceptional citrus fruit.

Tropical Sai Nam Peung Orange Smoothie

Ingredients: 2 1 cup Sai Nam Peung orange juice ½ cup coconut milk 1 ripe banana 1 cup frozen pineapple chunks Ice cubes (optional)

Instructions:

Blend the orange juice, coconut milk, banana, and pineapple in a blender until smooth. Add ice cubes if desired for a thicker consistency. Serve in a tall glass and enjoy! This tropical smoothie offers a creamy, indulgent way to enjoy Sai Nam Peung oranges.

Sai Nam Peung Orange & Basil Spritzer

Ingredients: 1 cup fresh Sai Nam Peung orange juice (about 3 oranges) 1 cup sparkling water 4-5 fresh basil leaves Ice cubes Orange slices for garnish

Instructions:

In a glass, muddle the basil leaves to release their aroma. Add the orange juice and stir well. Top with sparkling water and mix gently. Add ice cubes and garnish with an orange slice. This light, fizzy drink is perfect for summer gatherings or as a midday refresher

Sai Nam Peung Orange Mocktail

Ingredients: 2 Sai Nam Peung oranges, peeled and segmented 1 avocado, sliced 1/4 cup pomegranate seeds 2 cups mixed greens 2 tablespoons olive oil 1 tablespoon balsamic vinegar Salt and pepper to taste

Instructions:

Fill a glass with ice cubes. Pour in the orange juice and simple syrup, then top with soda water. Stir gently and garnish with fresh mint leaves. Serve chilled.

Sai Nam Peung Orange Margarita

Ingredients: 2½ cup Sai Nam Peung orange juice ¼ cup lime juice 2 oz tequila 1 oz triple sec Ice cubes Salt or sugar for rimming (optional) Orange slices for garnish

Instructions:

Rim the edge of a glass with salt or sugar. In a shaker, combine orange juice, lime juice, tequila, and triple sec with ice. Shake well and strain into the prepared glass. Garnish with an orange slice and enjoy responsibly. This zesty cocktail brings a burst of flavor to your next celebration.
